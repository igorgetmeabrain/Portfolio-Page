My Coding Adventure

Hi, I’m Doug.

A professional cellist by trade, the events of 2020 deprived me of my livelihood but afforded me the time to pursue new interests and to develop new skills.

I have not coded since my schooldays, when I learned rudimentary BASIC skills on a BBC Micro (when I wasn’t too busy playing Chuckie Egg).

I took the plunge during lockdown in July 2020, signing up at freeCodeCamp.

Since then, I have balanced the demands of a busy life with working on coding projects and certifications, and I hope soon to discover new and exciting career opportunities in the world of coding.

When I’m not coding or playing the cello, I’m also a keen chess player, speedcuber and family man.

Who knows where this will take me? I can’t wait to find out!
